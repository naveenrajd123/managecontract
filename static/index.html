üéØ Business Criticality

In today's business environment, contract management is not just administrative work‚Äîit's a strategic function that directly impacts your bottom line. Poor contract oversight can lead to:

  • Financial Loss: Missed renewal dates can result in unfavorable auto-renewals or service interruptions
  • Compliance Risks: Failing to meet contractual obligations can lead to penalties and legal disputes

üü° Medium Risk (~35%)

ü§ñ RAG (Retrieval Augmented Generation)

What is RAG? RAG combines information retrieval with generative AI to provide accurate, context-aware answers based on your actual contract documents.

RAG Pipeline:
  1. üìÑ Document Ingestion ‚Üí Extract text from contracts
  2. ‚úÇÔ∏è Text Chunking ‚Üí Split into semantic chunks (512-1024 tokens)
  3. üî¢ Embedding Generation ‚Üí Convert to 768-dimensional vectors
  4. üíæ Vector Storage ‚Üí Store embeddings for fast retrieval
  5. üîç Semantic Search ‚Üí Find relevant chunks using cosine similarity
  6. ü§ñ LLM Generation ‚Üí Generate answers based on retrieved context

üß† AI Model: Google Gemini 2.5 Flash

Why Gemini?

  • ‚ö° High Performance: Fast inference for real-time responses
  • üìù Large Context Window: 1M+ token context
  • üñºÔ∏è Multimodal: Can process text, PDFs, and images
  • üí∞ Cost-Effective: Optimized for production use at scale
